About Us

QwikFood is an online food delivery startup focusing on making home delivered food comfortable,fast and affordable. Founded in 2018, we are slowly growing to become South East Asia's largest food delivery startup. Headquartered at Kuala Lumpur.

QwikFood consists of small team of problem solvers and thinkers working around the clock to revolutinize the food delivery industry as well as to meet our mission and vision.

x

Our Mission

To bring good food into your everyday life. That means we just don't deliver--we bring it, always going the extra mile to make your experience memorable.

And it means this is delicious food you can enjoy everyday; from vibrant salads for healthy office lunches, to indulgent family-sized pizzas, to fresh sushi for a romantic night in. Whatever you crave, we can help.

Our Vision

To create an amazing takeaway experience and to revolutionize the restaurant industry by familiarizing the general public with the comfort of home delivered food.
